Adopt

If you're interested in adopting a kitten(s) please thoroughly read the following information.

Please only fill out the adoption application below if you are interested in a specific kitten(s) and 100% committed and ready to adopt from us.

We try our best to respond but due to the high volume of inquiries, our small adoption team and limited time, you may not receive a response.

Requirements

  • Unless specified otherwise, applicants must live within 4 hours driving distance of Cornwall, Ontario. In Canada only.
  • Applicants must be 21+ to apply.
  • Kittens are social animals and do better in pairs! Tiny but Mighty Kitten Rescue typically adopts out kittens in bonded pairs. If you do not have a friendly feline in your home, please consider applying for 2!
  • Fill out the form thoroughly. Incomplete adoption applications will not be considered.

What our adoption fee includes

Tiny but Mighty Kitten Rescue ensures that each cat / kitten is dewormed, treated for fleas/ear mites, vaccinated, spayed or neutered and microchipped.

Adult cats will receive their FVRCP and rabies vaccine. Some adult cats with existing medical conditions may not receive a rabies vaccine.

Kittens will receive their FVRCP vaccine. Adopters are responsible for their rabies vaccine after 16 weeks of age.

The adoption will not be finalized until they're sterilized and microchipped.

Adoption Fees

$200 for adult cats

$250 for 1 kitten

$400 for a pair

Our adoption fee helps to cover some of the costs for vet bills so we can continue to save lives.
