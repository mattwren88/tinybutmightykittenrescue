Wait and Watch: Observe the kittens from a safe distance for a few hours to see if their mother returns.

Check for Danger: Ensure the kittens are in a safe location, away from traffic, predators, and harsh weather.

Don't Disturb: Unless the kittens are in immediate danger, avoid picking them up or moving them.

If the Mother Doesn't Return: Contact your local animal shelter or rescue organization. If providing temporary care, keep them warm and quiet. Never feed cow's milk — use kitten formula. You can find helpful videos at kittenlady.org.
