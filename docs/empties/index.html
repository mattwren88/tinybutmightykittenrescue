Your Empties Can Help Save Lives

We cash in alcohol empties to help pay for life saving essentials and vet bills.

What We Accept

We accept any type of empty that previously contained alcohol: cans, bottles, wine bladders, etc.

Out of consideration for the time and energy of our small team of volunteers, we ask that you please rinse your alcohol empties to help avoid sticky messes at our drop off locations!

What We Do NOT Accept

Regular aluminum empties (soda cans / empty cat food cans), water bottles, juice boxes, cardboard, straws, garbage, etc.

Thank you for understanding!
