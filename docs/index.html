Our Mission

We are a registered charity that rescues orphaned kittens, offers pregnant cats a safe space to raise their final litter, provides full veterinary care and finds them forever homes. Through our subsidized spay and neuter program, we reduce the number of unwanted cats so fewer end up on the streets without homes.
